Type a short trigger code, get a full personalized DM template — like Magical, but built specifically for influencer outreach. Templates are editable before sending to avoid sounding robotic.

Adds a custom "Outreach Dashboard" button to the Google Sheets menu. Highlights due and overdue follow-ups in the sheet, then shows a sidebar with creator names, overdue counts, copyable templates, Open IG button, and one-click Mark as Sent / Unresponsive.
